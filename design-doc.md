# 🎨 Design Document — OM Export App

## 1. Design Philosophy

### Core Principles

* Premium & Trustworthy (export business credibility)
* Clean & Minimal (easy for global users)
* Visual-first (products are focus)
* Action-driven (inquiry is main goal)

---

## 2. Overall UI Direction

### Theme

* Primary: Dark Blue (#0B1F3A)
* Accent: Gold (#D4AF37)
* Background: White / Light Grey

### Style

* Modern + Luxury
* Inspired by real estate showcase UI
* High whitespace usage
* Large typography

---

## 3. Layout Structure

### 3.1 Homepage

#### Hero Section

* Full-width banner
* Background: Export ship / global trade visual
* Title: "Global Export Solutions"
* Subtitle: Short tagline
* CTA Button: "Explore Products"

#### Categories Section

* Grid layout (2–3 columns mobile, 4 desktop)
* Each card:

  * Icon/image
  * Category name

#### Featured Products

* Horizontal scroll cards
* Product image + short details

#### About Section

* Company intro
* Founder highlights

#### Contact CTA

* "Start Importing Today"

---

### 3.2 Product Listing Page

#### Layout

* Left: Filters (desktop)
* Right: Product grid

#### Features

* Search bar (top)
* Category filter
* Cards include:

  * Image
  * Product name
  * Short description

---

### 3.3 Product Detail Page (MOST IMPORTANT)

#### Hero Section (Inspired from Dribbble)

* Large product image (full width)
* Overlay text:

  * Product Name
  * Category
  * Short tagline

#### Product Info Section

* Grid layout:

  * Left: Image gallery
  * Right:

    * Specifications
    * MOQ
    * Origin
    * Packaging details

#### Inquiry Section (Primary CTA)

* Sticky or highlighted form
* Fields:

  * Name
  * Country
  * Quantity
  * Message
* Button: "Send Inquiry"

#### Trust Section

* "Exporting to 50+ Countries"
* Certifications / badges (future)

---

### 3.4 Contact Page

* Founder list with phone numbers
* Email contact
* Map (optional)

---

## 4. Components

### Buttons

* Primary: Gold background, dark text
* Secondary: Outline style

### Cards

* Rounded corners (12–16px)
* Soft shadow

### Typography

* Heading: Bold, large
* Body: Clean sans-serif

---

## 5. UX Decisions

### Why large images?

→ Builds trust and showcases product quality

### Why simple layout?

→ International users need clarity, not complexity

### Why strong CTA?

→ Goal is inquiry, not browsing

---

## 6. Mobile Responsiveness

* Stack layout vertically
* Sticky "Inquiry" button
* Swipeable product cards

---

## 7. Interaction Design

* Hover effects on cards
* Smooth transitions
* Image zoom on product click

---

## 8. Admin UI (Basic)

* Simple dashboard
* Table view for:

  * Products
  * Inquiries
* Add/Edit forms

---

## 10. Future Design Enhancements

* 3D product preview
* Video showcase

* Multi-language toggle

---

## 11. Summary

This design transforms a **property showcase concept** into a **product export platform**, focusing on:

* Visual storytelling
* Clear information hierarchy
* Strong conversion (inquiry-based)

The UI should feel premium, global, and trustworthy.
