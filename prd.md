# 📄 Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** OM Export App
**Company:** OM Export – Global Trade & Export Services

### Objective

Build a digital platform to manage, showcase, and facilitate export operations for multiple product categories. The app will connect global buyers, manage inquiries, and streamline export workflows.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Export businesses currently rely on manual communication methods like calls, WhatsApp, and email. This leads to:

* Poor tracking of inquiries
* No centralized product catalog
* Difficulty in scaling globally
* Inefficient communication with buyers

---

## 3. Goals

* Digitize export operations
* Build a global product catalog
* Enable buyer inquiries and lead generation
* Establish company credibility
* Improve communication efficiency

---

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

* International buyers / importers
* Distributors and wholesalers

### Secondary Users

* Admin (company team)
* Founders / Managers

---

## 5. Product Categories

The app will support the following export categories:

1. Dairy Products
2. Electronics
3. Readymade Garments
4. Cereals
5. Pharmaceuticals
6. Organic Chemicals
7. Petroleum Products
8. Textile Products
9. Mechanical Equipment
10. Jewellery

---

## 6. Key Features

### 6.1 Buyer Features

#### Product Catalog

* Browse products by category
* View product details:

  * Name
  * Description
  * Images
  * Specifications
  * MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)

#### Search & Filters

* Search by product name
* Filter by category

#### Inquiry System

* Submit inquiries for products
* Inquiry form fields:

  * Name
  * Country
  * Quantity
  * Message

#### Export Information

* Countries served
* Shipping and logistics details

#### Contact Section

* Direct contact via call/email
* Company information display

---

### 6.2 Admin Features

#### Product Management

* Add / Edit / Delete products
* Upload product images

#### Category Management

* Manage product categories

#### Inquiry Management

* View incoming inquiries
* Respond via email or WhatsApp

#### Founder Management

* Update founder details
* Manage contact information

## 7. User Flow

### Buyer Flow

1. Open the app
2. Browse categories
3. Select a product
4. View product details
5. Submit inquiry
6. Receive response

### Admin Flow

1. Login to admin panel
2. Manage products and categories
3. View inquiries
4. Respond to leads
